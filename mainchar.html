"CHARACTERS"
Takumi Fujiwara
Also known as the "White Ghost of Akina", Takumi uses his father's AE86 Trueno (Eight-six or "hachiroku") for tofu delivery. He is not interested in racing until he is forced into one against Keisuke Takahashi of the RedSuns.

Keisuke Takahashi
Originally a member of the RedSuns, he is the first person Takumi defeats in a race on Mt. Akina.

Ryosuke Takahashi
The elder of the Takahashi brothers is known across Gunma as the "White Comet." The leader of the Akagi RedSuns, he's more collected than his hotshot, hot-headed brother, but he's just as, if not more, determined to win.

Bunta Fujiwara
Tak's father enjoys a glorious past as a street racer. Now he's a single parent who has settled down as a tofu chef. But his old passion for racing may live on in his son. Bunta, like a wise sage, tacitly shapes Tak's racing skills.

Koichiro Iketani
The leader of the Akina Speed Stars discovers that there is still much to learn about racing, especially after he wrecks his beloved Silvia (a Nissan Silvia K's (S13)) trying to take the Akina downhill. Nobody is happier than he that Tak is taking racing more seriously.

Itsuki Takeuchi
Tak's best friend dreams of being a racer, a dream that collides with the reality of his limited skills. But he promises that one day he and Tak will form a downhill racing duo that will prove to be the best in the prefecture.

Yuuichi Tachibana
Long time friend of Bunta Fujiwara and the boss at the gas station that most of the Akina Speed Stars work at.

Natsuki Mogi
The cutest girl in school wants Tak as a boyfriend. But to get into this relationship, she'll need to let go of another one.

Kyoichi Sudo
The leader of Team Emperor is a one-time friend of Ry Takahashi, and the two share similar personalities: cool, aloof, extremely confident and highly skilled. Kyle is driven by the need to prove that he is better than Ry.

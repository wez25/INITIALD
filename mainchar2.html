"CHARACTERS"
Kyoko Iwase
A fellow FD racer that has eyes for K.T. She finds herself in an awkward position when she is elected by her team to race against him.

Kazumi Akiyama
If Wataru is gunning to steal Tak's racing crown, then his younger sister Kazumi has a different target-- Iggy's heart. She's a starry-eyed dreamer that befriends Iggy, whom instantly falls head over heels for her. She is searching for her place in the world, and finds Iggy to be much needed confidant.

Toshiya Joshima
Joshima is a living legend of street racing. His special one-arm driving technique earned him the nickname "Arm of God."

Kozo Hoshino
The other head of the Purple Shadow team and equally a legend in his own right. This old horse and Joshima go way back. For his skillful pedal work, he is also known as the "Leg of God."

Mako Sato
Mako is one half of the duo called Impact Blue along with Sayuki her navigator. Impact Blue's home course is Usui.

Sayuki
Sayuki is one half of the duo called Impact Blue along with Mako Sato her driver. Impact Blue's home course is Usui.
